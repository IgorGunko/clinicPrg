  • Speak to the booking team on +44 (0)20 7837 2905 (Mon – Fri 8.30am – 6pm)
  • Email us on info@crgh.co.uk
  • Complete the form below and a member of the team will be in touch.

Appointments last approximately one hour and are charged at £200. Each appointment is tailored to the needs of the individual but will typically include taking a full medical histo­ry.

If you would like further information before proceeding, you are very welcome to book a place at a patient information evening, or a ‘mini-consultation’ with a doctor, both of which are free of charge.

A mini-consultation gives you the opportunity to meet with one of our doctors, and get an overview of the treatments and services we offer. The appointment will last approximately 20 minutes. Mini-consultations are currently available on Tuesdays and Thursdays.
